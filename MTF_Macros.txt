ImageJ macros for calculating MTF from an image of a polished straight edge slanted at an angle of ~5-15 degrees:
  
  Slanted Edge MTF
  https://imagej.nih.gov/ij/plugins/se-mtf/index.html
  
  ASI_MTF
  https://github.com/emx77/ASI_MTF

Alternatively, if the beamstop is the only knife-edge available, try the following Linux software:
  
  FindDQE
  https://grigoriefflab.umassmed.edu/finddqe
